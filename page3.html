Different eSports

Rocket League

Rocket League is a Sports Action Game that involves driving rocket powered cars around as if they were soccar players in an attempt to hit the ball into a goal

Counter Strike Global Offensive

Counter Strike Global Offensive or CSGO for short is a first to 16 stragedy shooter that involves two sides, counter terrorists and terrorists. It is the terrorists job to either kill all of the counter terrorists or to detonate the bomb. It is the counter terrorists job to make sure the bomb is never planted or to make sure the bomb is defused if it is planted

League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA game that involves 5v5 gameplay where the main objective is to reach and detonate the enemy nexus.
